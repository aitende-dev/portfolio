My name is Anneke Iten de Leon. I was born and raised in Guatemala. I moved to Switzerland for my architectural studies, a step that opened up a new world for me, both geographically and personally. It was clear to me from an early age that I wanted to become an architect. This passion has stayed with me since childhood and has shaped my path to this day. During my studies, I was particularly drawn to the multidiscplinary nature of the subject. I specifically chose courses that connect different perspectives ranging from construction, 3D printing, and programming to design and philosophy. Literature has also been a valuable supplement for me. I am interested in how ideas take shape technically, creatively, and intellectually. For me, learning was never just a duty, but an opportunity to understand more deeply and to think across disciplines.
