Urban Housing

Location: London, UK

Year: 2024

Role: Lead Designer


This project explores the density of urban living within the historic fabric of London. Using local brick and stone, the facade mimics the rhythm of the existing streetscape while introducing modern apertures.

The interior concept focuses on open-plan living, utilizing light wells to penetrate the deep floor plan. The material palette consists of natural oak, poured concrete, and lime plaster.
