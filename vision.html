Ondertak is a contraction of the words onderdak (shelter) and tak (branch).
Both words refer to the vision and architecture that undertak stands for.

onderdak

The word onderdak means more than a roof over your head. Ondertak is also a place that offers peace, protection, and security. A place where people feel safe and can be themselves. A place that simultaneously invites encounter and is in contact with the surroundings. Ondertak wants to design such places.

Ondertak builds places where the meeting between inside and outside is not an afterthought, but a goal. Every design seeks a fine balance between security and openness, between silence and encounter. Light and air, sun and shade, different seasons, greenery, and surroundings are essential parts of a good design.

tak

The word tak refers to the organic and to growth. The branch is a part of a larger whole. That is also true for a design.

At Ondertak, every project grows in dialogue. Listening, inspiring, bringing together, and guiding belong to the design and construction process. Building or renovating is not something you do every year, so the run-up to it deserves a personal framework. Ondertak offers space to search together, to dig deeper, and to find solutions that embody who you are and how you want to live.

Homes grow not only during design and construction, but also afterwards. Homes have a lifespan. They move with current and future residents. Intergenerational building sounds big, but works practically. This happens by laying out water drainage for a shower on the ground floor, a storage room that can become a fully-fledged workplace if necessary, a wall that can possibly be removed to divide a space differently later...
