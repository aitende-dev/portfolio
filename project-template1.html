Urban Housing Collective

University • 3rd Year Thesis

The Concept

This project explores the relationship between high-density living and communal green spaces. Located in the heart of Berlin, the structure uses a modular timber grid to allow for flexible apartment layouts.

The core challenge was maintaining privacy while encouraging social interaction in the shared corridors. We utilized a staggered balcony system to create visual connections without compromising individual space.
